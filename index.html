We come in peace...literally

Poster Invasion is a woman-owned social enterprise. We work with artists all over the world to bring their work to their generation in print form. We run large-scale turnkey pop-up poster sale events on campuses with 200-35,000+ students. We are based in Greenville, SC and we operate in 40+ states!
